Prishtina

The Capital City Of Kosovo

Prishtina is the capital and largest city of Kosovo. Its population is predominantly Albanian-speaking, constituting the second-largest such city in Europe, after Tirana. The city is located in the northeastern section of Kosovo in a relatively flat plain close to the Gollak mountains.
